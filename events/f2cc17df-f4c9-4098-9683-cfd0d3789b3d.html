Annual May Day Festival

Haledon, NJ

The American Labor Museum proudly hosts its "Annual May Day Festival" on Thursday, May 1st, 2025 at 7PM. Plans call for instrumental and vocal performances of folk and labor music by George Mann, accompanied by Marty Confurius and Al Podber. Annamaria Stefanelli, lyric soprano, will sing opera arias that were popular among immigrant workers of the early 1900's. The New Jersey Industrial Union Council's "Solidarity Singers" will also perform featuring creative original lyrics and familiar melodies. Join us as we celebrate the international workers' holiday.
